About Our Firm

With a legacy of excellence and a commitment to client success, LexPrime Legal Group has been at the forefront of legal representation in New York since 2005.

Founded in 2005, LexPrime Legal Group has established itself as a premier law firm in New York City, dedicated to providing exceptional legal services with a focus on achieving tangible results for our clients.

Our team of experienced attorneys brings together diverse expertise and a shared commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients based on trust and successful outcomes.

At LexPrime, we understand that legal matters can be complex and stressful. That's why we prioritize clear communication, strategic thinking, and personalized attention to every case we handle.

Our track record speaks for itself - we've successfully represented clients in high-stakes litigation, complex corporate transactions, and sensitive family matters, always with the same dedication to achieving the best possible outcome.

Our Practice Areas

We offer comprehensive legal services across multiple practice areas, providing our clients with the expertise they need for their specific legal challenges.

Family Law

Comprehensive legal services for divorce, child custody, adoption, prenuptial agreements, and other family-related matters with compassion and expertise.

Corporate Law

Strategic counsel for businesses of all sizes, including formation, contracts, mergers, acquisitions, intellectual property, and compliance matters.

Real Estate Law

Expert guidance on residential and commercial real estate transactions, leasing, zoning, property disputes, and development projects.

Criminal Defense

Aggressive defense representation for individuals facing criminal charges, protecting rights and seeking favorable outcomes through negotiation or trial.

Personal Injury

Dedicated representation for victims of accidents, medical malpractice, and wrongful death, fighting for the compensation you deserve.

Estate Planning

Comprehensive estate planning services including wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and probate administration to protect your legacy.
